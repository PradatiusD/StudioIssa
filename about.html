[/two_sixths_first][four_sixths]Stephanie is the founder and primary creative consultant at Studio Issa. Her pursuit for showcasing innovative individuals and concepts began in the summer of 2010 in Beirut, Lebanon, where she worked on CRTDA’s Arab Women’s Right to Nationality Campaign. While pursuing her education in New York she excelled as a Public Relations intern for Time and Fortune Magazines, assisting in Time’s “Person of the Year Panel” and “Best Inventions of the YEAR” releases on MSNBC’s “The Today Show” and was involved with publishing and editing video packages on CNNMoney.com. Since then, Stephanie continues to obtain clients who focus on strategic communication and messaging. She is traveler with a passion for foreign affairs, women’s rights, communication and design.[/four_sixths]

Jay Gidwitz

[four_sixths_first]As a lead designer and visual artist for Studio Issa, Jay is dedicated to over-lapping his fine art background into his visual design projects. Since 2008, Jay has been working at the Creative Arts Council at Brown University’s Granoff Center, where he worked as a photographer and videographer. After earning his degree in classical drawing and painting at Chicago’s School of Representational Art, Jay went on to receive his B.A. in Visual Art at Brown University where he focused on photography, digital art and web design. He is the founder of Surrealism Today, a contemporary surrealism blog and runs the largest Surrealism group on Vimeo. Jay’s art portfolio is visible at JayGidwitz.com.[/four_sixths_first][two_sixths]
